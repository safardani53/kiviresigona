BLAC Chyna has broken down in court over nude "revenge porn" photos that her ex Rob Kardashian once posted online, The Sun can report.

Chyna, 33, took the stand on Thursday morning as part of her $100M lawsuit against Rob's mother, Kris Jenner, and sisters Kim, Khloe and Kylie for allegedly getting her E! reality show canceled in 2017.

Michael Rhodes, the Kardashians' attorney, continued his cross-examination today, and referenced when Chyna filed for a restraining order against Rob in 2017 after he shared nude photos of her on Instagram.

Chyna was asked to look at her declaration in court, which included the x-rated images, and she became visibly upset, according to The Sun's reporter.

The model's voice broke as she asked for a break, and she began rubbing her eyes.

Chyna's lawyer, Lynne Ciani, was outraged the nude photos were brought up in the courtroom, and demanded they not be shown to the jury.

"Rob put nude pics of me on the internet," she added, claiming she wanted to stop him from causing her further embarrassment.

The judge then called for a short break for Chyna to collect herself.

Rob's mom Kris and sisters Khloe and Kim were also in the Los Angeles courtroom today, while Kylie was not in attendance.
