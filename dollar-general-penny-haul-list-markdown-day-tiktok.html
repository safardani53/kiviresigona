A SMART customer came home with a shopping haul of over 20 items from Dollar General, paying only a penny for each of them.

Here are the exact days to find this kind of deal each week.

Ahmazing Gray shares mind-blowing discounts on her TikTok account which has nearly 40,000 followers.

In a recent video, she shared her “penny haul” from Dollar General, showing off a shopping cart full of products.

The poster only paid a penny for each of the items.

Penny items are specific items found at Dollar General that the store has a hard time selling.

These items have usually already been heavily discounted - sometimes by more than 90 percent.

To make them more easily identifiable, the items are marked down to $0.01 so the staff knows to remove them from the sale floor.

However, these items can be missed, making it easier for customers to find these products on store shelves.

If you’re on the lookout for these heavily discounted items, the Krazy Coupon Lady says these markdowns usually happen on Tuesdays.

Be sure to also pay attention to the size, flavor, and count of the item you’re purchasing as everything must match with the penny items to get the $0.01 price.

According to the Krazy Coupon Lady website, some Dollar General stores may not let you purchase these items once they ring up as $0.01 at the register.

“Remember, these are items that the employees should have already pulled from the shelf,” read the website.

“Don’t even talk to the employees about the penny items. And please, please, please … don’t call corporate about a penny item situation.”

To check the price of an item before you head to the register, you can use the Dollar General mobile app to price scan any items.

Be sure that your version of the app is the newest one available before going to the store - this will ensure you’re getting the most up-to-date prices.
