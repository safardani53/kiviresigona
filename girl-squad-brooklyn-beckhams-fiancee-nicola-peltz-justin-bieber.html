Meet the super-rich girl squad of Brooklyn Beckhams fiancee Nicola Peltz - and Justin Bieber know

February 22, 2024
blog

SHE’S blessed with model looks and comes from one of the richest families in the US.

But when Nicola Peltz walks down the aisle with new fiance Brooklyn Beckham, she’s likely to be followed by a gaggle of equally beautiful and wealthy bridesmaids.

The 25-year-old counts famous model Gigi Hadid, a rock star and the daughter of soul singer Lionel Richie among her girl squad.

This weekend, Brooklyn, 21, announced he'd popped the question with an incredible £250,000 ring after dating the stunning actress for two years.

The wedding unites two super-rich families, with Nicola’s dad Nelson said to be worth £1.3billion and Brooklyn’s famous parents, David and Victoria Beckham estimated to be worth £335million - so it’s bound to be a lavish affair.

So just who are the lucky ladies in the bride-to-be’s inner circle?

Gigi Hadid

Like Nicola, Gigi grew up in a fabulously wealthy family with property developer dad Mohamed thought to have been worth £320million in his heyday.

The girls, who are both 25, started modelling in their teens and became close very quickly.

Nicola appeared in a music video for Zan Malik, Gigi’s boyfriend, in 2016.

She told Teen Vogue: "Gigi really wanted me to do it. I love her so much and I‘m really close with her, but yeah, she was excited for me. She’s the best."

Nicola also dated Gigi’s brother Anwar for a year, from 2017, and the girls’ relationship suffered a blip when they split, after he was caught kissing Kendall Jenner.

But they have moved past the hiccup and remain friends.

Sofia Richie

Lionel Richie’s youngest daughter has also lived a lavish life, growing up in a £9million mansion in Bel Air.

Her famous dad, who had a string of hits including Dancing on the Ceiling and Hello in the 1980s, is estimated to be worth £160million.

Michael Jackson was her godfather and, as a child, she would hang out with his daughter Paris at the now infamous Neverland ranch in Colorado.

The model, now 21, has been Nicola’s best friend since 2015 and they are often seen together at fashion shows, meeting for lunch at LA eatery Mauro’s and going bowling.

Sofia, who is dating Kourtney Kardashian’s ex Scott Disick, enjoys a jet set lifestyle, posting pictures of beaches and stunning hotels in Mexico and the Caribbean as well as skiing holidays in Aspen.

As well as a modelling career and rich daddies, the pair also have brief flings with Justin Bieber in common.

Sofia dated the singer briefly when she was 17 and Nicola went on a few dates with him in June 2016.

Cindy Kimberly

If swapping Justin Bieber anecdotes is the key to Nicola’s inner circle then her other bestie, Cindy 'Wolfie' Kimberly is well qualified.

Unlike others in the group the Spanish model doesn’t come from a privileged background, but was brought up by a single mum who worked long hours to put food on the table.

But life changed dramatically when the Canadian singer spotted her on Instagram and posted her picture with the caption, “OMG who is this?”

Model agencies were soon beating a path to her door and the 21-year-old is now one of the most sought after beauties in the business.

Since she shot to fame, and after dating Justin she has been linked with Lewis Hamilton and rapper Tyga.

She and Nicola are very close, constantly posting Instagram pictures together on both their accounts.

Caroline Daur

A love of fashion is behind Nicola’s friendship with German fashion blogger Caroline ‘Caro’ Daur.

The 25-year-old founded Carodaur.com in 2014 and now has 2.4million followers and a net worth of £4million.

The Hamburg- born influencer also has partnerships with numerous brands, including Dolce & Gabbana, Adidas and Cartier.

Pyper America Smith

Mormon model and musician Pyper America Smith rose to catwalk fame in 2016, modelling for Dolce & Gabbana and Georgio Armani.

When she’s not strutting her stuff on the runway, she’s the bassist in the alternative rock band The Atomics.

The 23-year-old dated Brandon Lee, son of Pamela Anderson and Tommy Lee, before marrying Australian Quaid Rippon Holder last year.

She’s said to have a net worth of around £5million.
