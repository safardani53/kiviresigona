MEGHAN Markle has revealed that little Archie has developed a new hobby - but he wasn’t very impressed when she bought him a present to encourage him.

The Duchess of Sussex, 42, shared how her pal Misan Harriman, who is a filmmaker, had been teaching her four-year-old son about photography.

The royal spoke to Misan and actor David Oyelowo in a post-screening chat for their short film, The After, and gave some sweet details about Prince Archie.

Meghan gushed about Misan inspiring her son, saying: "Inspiration runs deep. 

“Our four-and-a-half-year-old son – he would say 'I'm not four, I'm four and a half' – Misan was showing him how to do photography the last time he was with us.”

The duchess shared how she had kindly bought Archie a camera to encourage his passion, but it hadn’t gone down well.
