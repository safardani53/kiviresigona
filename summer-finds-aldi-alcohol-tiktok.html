I found eight summer finds at Aldi starting at $5.99 including a premade essential

March 15, 2024
blog

ALDI has expanded its grocery offerings by stocking shelves with adult beverages.

A TikToker recently covered eight of their favorite Aldi alcohol finds.

In a video by Katie Kelly (@bitsbitesblog), a food and Aldi blogger, the blogger points out eight different alcoholic drinks available in select stores.

"If your Aldi does not have alcohol, I am truly so sorry to hear that," Kelly says at the beginning of the video.

"But if yours does, here are some really great summer Aldi alcohol finds."

Prices for the drinks may vary by store.

Here are Kelly's suggestions:

1. OH ME, OH MY! MIMOSA

Aldi stocked the shelves with an $8.99 pineapple-flavored mimosa.

The company said the mimosa is made with real fruit juice.

"That pineapple mimosa is so good," Kelly suggested.

2. GIAMBELLINO WATERMELON BELLINI

The summery watermelon drink was brought to store shelves for a limited time.

The wine and fruit juice mix has an eight percent alcohol content.

"That looked phenomenal," Kelly said.

The watermelon flavor is $5.99.

3. GIAMBELLINO PINEAPPLE BELLINI

Like the watermelon-flavored Bellini, the pineapple flavor has eight percent alcohol.

"The vibrant wine cocktail is made with fresh pineapples for a perfect mix of tangy and sweet," Aldi said about the drink.

The pineapple is on sale for $5.99.

4. PUNCH BOWL COCKTAIL

Aldi offers two Punch Bowl brand wine cocktails - the Strawberry Daiquiri and the Lemonade flavors.

"The semi-sweet wine cocktail bursts with delicious strawberry and rum flavor in every sip," the website said about the Daiquiri flavor.

Both drinks are $9.99 for a 1.5-liter bottle.

5. DON'T MIND IF I DO

The TikToker pointed out the Don't Mind If I Do brand White Chardonnay.

Aldi said the drink is gluten and sugar-free.

The 750ml bottle will set customers back $7.99.

6. INTERMINGLE WINE

Intermingle brand has two wines on Aldi shelves - a Red Blend and a Buttery Chardonnay.

Both wines are just $6.99.

"The crisp white wine is light and refreshing for a smooth sip every time," Aldi said about the Chardonnay.

On the Red Blend, the company said: "The unique mix combines the sweetness of ripe berries with decadent, richer notes."

7. SUNSHINE BAY SAUVIGNON BLANC

Aldi imported the Sunshine Bay wine from New Zealand.

"It has tropical guava and passion fruit flavors that are balanced perfectly with crisp acidity, stony minerality, and citrus notes," the company noted.

The bottle is worth $7.95.

8. ZARITA PRE-MADE DRINKS

Aldi stocks five flavors of the premade mixes.

Strawberry Margarita, Mango Margarita, Grapefruit Paloma, Lime Margarita, and Blueberry Margarita are all available in select stores.

The drink mixes will set customers back $13.99.
