DWTS fans have slammed guest judge Michael Buble after he made an awkward and "inappropriate" comment to Charli D'Amelio.

The uncomfortable moment came after the TikTok star and her partner Mark Ballas performed on Monday night.

Charli, 18, and Mark danced a Foxtrot routine to one of Michael's cover of the hit song, Fever, on the most recent episode of DWTS and then stood before the panel of judges awaiting their results.

Michael, 47, shocked viewers with his notes, as he said to the teen: "I'm not telling you this, Charli, because I want you to love me like you love Len.

"But I actually wrote this down...effortless, mesmerizing, and expressively erotic."

The teen social media sensation appeared noticeably uncomfortable by the comment, as her face can be seen immediately cringing while her partner Mark stood in disbelief.
