Engaged! Ready to See the Ring?

April 04, 2024
blog

Emily VanCamp and Josh Bowman portrayed occasional lovers on Revenge.

But the gorgeous actress and very handsome husband have now agreed to make the fictional into the non-fictional, the sporadic into the permanent.

Meaning what, exactly?

They just got engaged!!!!!!!

The former co-stars made the announcement in a somewhat subtle but also very romantic manner:

VanCamp shared the following photo on her Instagram page last night, including with it a simple heart emoji as a caption.

Because what else is there to say, right?

That is most definitely an engagement ring and those, we can only presume, are tears of joy that the actress is covering up with her hands.

VanCamp and Bowman were two of the main leads on ABC’s Revenge, a drama that ran from 2011 through 2015.

The former portrayed Emily Thorne, a young woman intent on destroying the lives of those who framed her father for an act of terrorism.

Bowman played Daniel Grayson, a key member of the family on whom Emily set her vengeful sights.

Neither star has talked much in public about their romance with the other, but that doesn’t mean they’ve remained completely mum.

"I have a wonderful love in my life,” VanCamp told InStyle Australia two years ago, for instance, adding at the time:

"Trust is the most important thing in any relationship, because everything falls into place after that."

Aware of the pitfalls that can endanger many co-stars who turn into couples, VanCamp also told Elle Canada in 2014 that she and Bowman try to keep the professional separated from the personal.

"Josh and I keep it all very separate," she said, continuing as follows:

"I barely talk about him in interviews, to be honest. It’s just really funny in the moments when we’re playing these absolutely absurd things. I call him ‘wife-beater Daniel.’

"It’s hard for my family to watch – it’s also hard for them to see me in that light. But we’re actors. None of that lives in any of us. Thank God!"
