This Plastic Surgeon Completely Transformed His Wife

September 13, 2024
blog

Operation: Build the perfect partner. Phillip Craft, a plastic surgeon from Miami, has been reconstructing his wife Anna’s body for the past 11 years — and he’s still not finished.

Though Anna, 43, initially turned down her husband’s requests for her to have cosmetic surgery, she had a change of heart after the birth of their second son in 2005. “I felt my figure wasn’t the same. I was helping Phillip out at work, and I’d see all these gorgeous 60-year-old women with rocking bodies walking out of the practice,” the mother of Aston, 13, and Philip Jr., 11, told News Dog Media in an interview published Tuesday, November 22. “That’s when I thought, ‘Why not?’ I wanted to feel better about myself.”

So at the age of 32, Anna had a breast augmentation followed by liposuction, butt enhancements, body-contouring surgery, waist and abdominal sculpting and Botox. Anna’s plump pout and apple cheeks are courtesy of fillers.

“Now when Anna wears her small bikinis, everyone stops to look at her curves,” Phillip gushed to News Dog Media. “After pregnancy, Mother Nature made everything go south, so I plumped her buttocks. Most importantly, I gave Anna her mojo back.”

Anna, who manages Phillip’s practice, told the British media outlet, “I’m the epitome of my husband’s work.” He agreed: “When clients come in, I say, ‘My wife is my in-house model.’”

The couple, who have been married for 21 years, met in 1995 in the gift shop of the hospital where Phillip was studying medicine. “Anna had bent over to pick up a card she’d just dropped. I saw her beautiful legs, and from that moment, I knew I’d be hooked for life,” he told News Dog Media. But he admitted “after my surgical tweaks to her body, I think she’s even more beautiful now.”
