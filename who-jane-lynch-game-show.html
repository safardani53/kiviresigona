Who is Jane Lynch?

Lynch was born on July 14, 1960, in Evergreen Park, Illinois.

The actress attended both Illinois State University and Cornell University.

She earned her MFA degree from Cornell University in 1984.

When it comes to her romantic life, Lynch has been married twice.

Lynch and her ex Lara Embry were married from 2010 to 2014.

In 2021, she married her partner Jennifer Cheyne in a small courthouse wedding, People reports.

At the time, she appeared on Live with Kelly and Ryan to talk about her marriage.

"We've been together a really long time. It was more touching than I was expecting," she said.

The Glee alum is an award-winning actress including an Emmy for Outstanding Supporting Actress In A Comedy Series.

She has received a total of 13 Emmy Award nominations and has won five times.

Her most recent win was for Outstanding Guest Actress In A Comedy Series in 2019.

Lynch won the award for her role as Sophie Lennon in the Amazon series The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel.

Who is Jane Lynch's Wife, Jennifer?

Jennifer Cheyne is a screenwriter who studied at UC Berkeley.

She wrote the screenplay for Mary Astor's Diary, a movie that has twice placed in the second round of the Austin Film Festival.

In 2019, Jane revealed to Parade that the two dated in the early 2000s.

“Jennifer and I were partners for a couple of years in the early 2000s,” she said.

“We broke up and then reconnected a decade later and just fell back together so easily and lovingly.”
