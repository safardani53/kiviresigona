VALENTINE'S Day gifts can fall into a few different categories: romantic, lavish, sweet (yes, we're talking about chocolate) and funny.

Funny Valentine's gifts are the best - they don't cost a lot, they work whether you've been together two minutes or 20 years and they celebrate how well you get each other as a couple.

After all, making someone laugh is surely the secret to everlasting love, right?

There are lots of options when you're looking for a funny Valentine's gift, and, happily, most are under £15.

A funny mug is a good choice for the avid tea or coffee drinker - you know your gift will get used all the time, and each sip will bring a smile to your loved one's face.

Foodie gifts can also be funny: give them something they've never had before, like a cocktail in a sweet courtesy of Smith & Sinclair, or play on words by giving your hottie something extra hot.

Of course, Valentine's Day wouldn't be Valentine's Day without something a little sexy, so we've thrown one of those ideas in here too.
