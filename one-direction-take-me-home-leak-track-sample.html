One Direction Take Me Home Leak New Album Released Early Online Hollywood Life

July 22, 2024
blog

Samples of the songs from the entire album have been leaked almost two weeks before their intended debut!

The leaked tracks include “Kiss You,” “C’mon,C’mon,” “Last First Kiss,” “Heart Attack,” “Rock Me,” “Back For You,” “Change My Mind,” “I Would,” “Over Again,” and “They Don’t Know About Us.”

Furthermore, samples of additional tracks from the deluxe version, Take Me Home: Yearbook Edition, also leaked. (It’s like Christmas came early!)

One fan tweeted, “Listening to the leak snip bits of ‘Take Me Home’. I NEED THE ALBUM NOW. 😍 Love itttttt.”

UPDATE: Perhaps due to the early leak, iTunes is now offering fans the opportunity to stream the full album a week before its release, starting today Nov. 5!

According a recent press release, upcoming appearances include: a morning television first as the band returns to NBC’s the Today show plaza where viewers will be treated to two days of outdoor concerts on November 13 and 14. They’ll perform tracks from their new album TAKE ME HOME as well as their debut album UP ALL NIGHT. Fans will also get a sneak peek of One Direction when they perform on The X Factor on November 8!
