Craig Harris Carton is an American radio personality known as the co-host of the sports radio show Carton and Roberts on the WFAN network in New York City. He was arrested in September 2017 for securities fraud and wire fraud, before re-signing his contract with the station in October 2020 after serving his sentence. On March 29, 2018, in the midst of his legal battles, Carton launched his new show, "Carton and Friends" starring Michelle Serpico and Corey Parsons, the FNTSY Sports Network, an online broadcaster. In November 2018, Carton was found guilty on all counts. He was sentenced to three and a half years in prison and three years of probation and paid $ 4.8 million in damages. Since his release from prison, Carton has been hired by WFAN, where he currently hosts an afternoon driving program with Evan Roberts.
