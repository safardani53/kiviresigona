Oprah Winfrey Watch What Happens Live: Jokes About Her Big Breasts

March 08, 2024
blog

Oprahcalypse has hit Watch What Happens Live! Promoting her new movie The Butler, Oprah Winfrey joined host Andy Cohen in the Bravo Clubhouse on Thursday, Aug. 15. The 59-year-old TV legend sipped on a vodka tonic while answering Cohen's tough questions and playing the show's hilarious games.

The late night host asked Winfrey what she thought about her Butler costar Terrence Howard's comment about her "tig ol' bitties.'"

"It really is okay because they really are big," Winfrey said. "The other day I went, 'God, are y'all getting bigger?' These are my grandmother's — she's with me."

Cohen later joked, "Not only does she have a bountiful garden, she has bountiful . . . " Winfrey quipped, "Breastseses!"

Also during the show, Winfrey indulged Cohen by screaming his name in her classic Oprah style and played his Oprah Encycl-O-pedia game to test her "Oprah" knowledge.

In more a serious moment during the show, Cohen asked Winfrey, on behalf of a viewer's Facebook question, if she wishes she had been tougher on Whitney Houston during their 2009 interview.

"No, I don't. You know why, because I do the same thing with all important interviews — I did this the other day with Lindsay Lohan — before I sit down with them I have a little private moment . . . and I say, 'Tell me what you want to get out of this interview.'" Winfrey recalls the late singer telling her, "'I just want people to know the truth. I'm tired of all the bulls–t. And I want people to know the real me and see the real me.' And I go, 'Okay, I can help you do that.' So I would like to know where [people] think I didn't take her to task."
