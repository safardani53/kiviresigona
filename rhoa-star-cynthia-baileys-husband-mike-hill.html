FORMER model and Real Housewives of Atlanta star Cynthia Bailey and her husband Mike Hill have been married since October 2020.

Hill has two children from his pervious relationship in Ashlee and Kayla, while Bailey has a 21-year-old daughter in Noelle Robinson.

Who is RHOA star Cynthia Bailey's husband Mike Hill?

Born August 19, 1970, Mike Hill is a television personality and talk show host currently signed with Fox Soul and Fox Sports.

Hill was born in the Bronx, and spent his childhood between Bessemer, Alabama and New York City.

He enlisted as a reservist in the US Air Force after graduating high school.

Hill began his career as a sports director at WHAG-TV in Hagerstown, Maryland, in 1995.

He got his first anchoring job later that year with KSEE-TV in Fresno, California.

Hill was with ESPN from 2004 to 2013, where he hosted Hill and Schlereth with Mark Schlereth on ESPN Radio.

He also hosted a variety of the networks shows including SportsCenter, ESPN First Take, NFL Live, Baseball Tonight and various other remote projects.

In August 2013, he joined Fox Sports 1 where he has guest hosted Fox Sports Live and Fox Football Daily.

Hill and Bailey got engaged in July 2019, and officially married on October 10, 2020.

What is Mike Hill's net worth?

As of 2020, Mike Hill has estimated a net worth of $1.5million.

His earnings as a sportscaster are in the range of $10,000 to $230,000.

When did Hill and Bailey have their wedding ceremony?

The 53-year-old reality star got hitched in an extravagant wedding ceremony with 250 guests in attendance, including RHOA co-stars Kenya Moore and Kandi Burruss.
