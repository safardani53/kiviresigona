Shop our top picks at Trinny London

1. Miracle Blur

The Miracle Blur is one of Trinny London's original launches and one which has stood the test of time and remains popular with shoppers years later.

The Miracle Blur is a huge hit because it blurs and fills any fine lines, wrinkles, pores and acne scars, with ease, so you can apply your make-up products seamlessly on top for a flawless finish and youthful appearance.

2. Overnight Sensation

We love new lotions and potions, and our favourite skincare essential is an overnight moisturiser to give your skin some TLC during your slumber.

We should be asleep for at least eight hours at night, but eight hours without any moisturiser or hydration can leave your skin looking dull, dry and lacklustre come the morning.

The solution: Trinny London's Overnight Sensation.

This skincare saviour contains retinal+ as well as retinoid, which work to hydrate, plump, exfoliate and brighten the skin.

3. Lash2Brow

We love a multipurpose make-up cosmetic, and the Lash2Brow is the dream creation we are head over heels for.

Whether you love a bushy brow or voluminous lashes, this genius two-in-one can help you perfect both, without taking up too much space in your beauty bag.

One end features a miniature brush perfect to run through your eyebrows, add a hint of colour and brush them to accentuate the feature.

While the other end has a longer bristle brush for your lashes.

4. Golden Glow

We love a bronzer, and simply will not leave the house without it.

Whether you are going full glam, or want a simple natural make-up look, the Golden Glow is the dream purchase.

This cream bronzer is subtle, blendable and lightweight, so you can warm your cheeks, accentuate your bone structure with a spot of contouring using this bargain buy.

It comes in four shades so there is bound to be a colour to suit your skin tone.

We also recommend sweeping a touch across your eyelids too.

5. Lip2Cheek

We've said it before, and we will say it again, we love a multipurpose beauty product.

Not only does it make it super easy for beginner beauty buffs, but great for those looking to cut down the contents of their beauty bag when on the go.

This Lip2Cheek essential is perfectly pigmented to apply on your cheeks and lips.

The creamy formula is blendable, but also longlasting, so you can enhance the colour on your face for those wanting an extra pop of colour.

6. BFF SPF

The BFF SPF is another firm favourite for many because it is a nourishing moisturiser, which protects your skin, but also provides a natural glow, and blurs any hyperpigmentation too.

It's the perfect base in your skincare routine, which you can apply a full face of make-up on tip, or wear on its own.

This product is infused with microencapsulated pigments, which blend with your skin tone to reduce any redness, smooth your skin and balance your complexion for a flawless finish.
