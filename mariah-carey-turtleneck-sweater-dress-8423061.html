Mariah Carey Wore a Turtleneck Sweater Dress in Aspen

March 29, 2024
blog

If there were ever an official authority on winter fashion, it would be the Queen of Christmas herself, Mariah Carey. 

The singer held onto the holiday spirit a little longer with her recent stay in snowy Aspen, where you can find most skiers in puffer coats and toboggans. But not Carey. She stayed elevated, temperature- and style-wise, in a long sweater dress in a creamy taupe color. 

The cable knit maxi dress reached the singer’s ankles, keeping her extra warm from the elements with help from its turtleneck and long sleeves. To keep her ears warm and long coils defined, she opted for a very on-brand pair of white fuzzy muffs. Pops of black in the form of oversized shades and heeled booties completed her look.

Dressing up and staying warm is a seasonal challenge that arises every year, and with a turtleneck sweater dress, Carey, along with other celebs, like Kate Middleton and Taylor Swift, who wore their own versions of the trend, may have just cracked the code. They offer warmth with their full-body coverage, while the bodycon shape keeps them out of frumpy territory. Best of all, wearing one feels like being wrapped in a cozy blanket. 

We don’t know the exact dress Carey wore, so we rounded up a few comparable sweater dresses below starting at $36.
