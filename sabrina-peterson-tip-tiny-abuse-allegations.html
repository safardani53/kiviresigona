Who is Sabrina Peterson?

Sabrina Peterson is an alleged close family friend of rapper T.I. and his songstress wife Tiny, although those claims have not been confirmed.

She is a hemp farm business owner and runs Pretty High Co. which sells hemp flowers, CBD tea, and other related products.

Peterson's products have been featured in Forbes, Essence, and Black Enterprise.

She boasts 246,000 followers on Instagram and is an outspoken activist for black rights.

What has Sabrina Peterson accused T.I. and Tiny of?

Under the social media handle theglamuniversity, Peterson made claims of sexual misconduct against T.I. on Instagram saying that in the past, he threatened her with a gun.

T.I.'s wife Tameka similarly took to Instagram to dismiss the sensational claims.

T.I. and Tiny's spokesperson released a statement on their behalf on January 29, 2021, denying all claims made against them.

The statement read: "Mr and Mrs Harris want to be on record and more importantly want the public to know they emphatically deny in the strongest way possible the egregiously appalling allegations being made against them by Sabrina Peterson.

"The Harrises have had difficulty with this woman for well over a decade. They are taking this matter very seriously."
