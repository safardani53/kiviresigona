Rihanna and A$AP Rocky's Relationship Timeline

April 15, 2024
blog

It's safe to say Rihanna and A$AP Rocky both have "Love on the Brain!"

The musical pair were close friends for nearly a decade before their longtime friendship blossomed into a romantic relationship. Since PEOPLE confirmed their relationship in November 2020, the couple has been spotted out together frequently, on dates and on vacation together.

On May 19, 2022, PEOPLE confirmed that Rihanna and A$AP Rocky welcomed their first child, RZA Athelston. "Rihanna is doing well," a source told PEOPLE. "They are very excited to be parents. Rihanna is already a wonderful mom."

In February 2023, Rihanna revealed she was pregnant with her second child as she debuted her baby bump at the Super Bowl Halftime show. In August, sources confirmed to PEOPLE that she and A$AP Rocky had welcomed their second child, Riot Rose Mayers.

Read on to see the evolution of Rihanna and A$AP Rocky's romantic history, from a sultry performance at the 2012 MTV VMAs to welcoming their two children.

September 6, 2012: Rihanna and A$AP Rocky collaborate and perform together

Nearly a decade ago, RiRi may not have been penning a "Loveeeeeee Song" for Rocky, but the pair's friendship was kicking off. The singer had the rapper record a verse for the remix of her song "Cockiness (Love It)"; the pair then put on a steamy show while performing the song during the 2012 MTV Video Music Awards, showing off their chemistry even then.

March 2013: A$AP Rocky joins Rihanna on tour

The rapper, born Rakim Mayers, served as the opening act during the North American leg of Rihanna's Diamonds World Tour.

September 25, 2013: Rihanna stars in A$AP Rocky's music video

Life imitates art! Seven years before they'd officially become an item, the pals once again proved they'd make a gorgeous pair when they portrayed a couple for the rapper's "Fashion Killa" music video.

June 2018: Rihanna and A$AP Rocky attend Paris Fashion Week together

Fast-forwarding a bit: the longtime pals made a strikingly stylish pair in coordinating black-and-white outfits while sitting front row at the Louis Vuitton Menswear Spring/Summer 2018 show together during Paris Fashion Week.

September 13, 2018: A$AP Rocky attends Rihanna's annual Diamond Ball

As longtime friends, it came as no surprise to see Rocky on the guestlist for Rihanna's annual Diamond Ball, which serves as one of fashion's biggest nights and a fundraiser for her Clara Lionel Foundation.

September 18, 2019: A$AP Rocky returns to Rihanna's Diamond Ball

The "L.S.D." rapper made an appearance at Rihanna's annual fête for the second year in a row in 2019.

December 2, 2019: Rihanna and A$AP Rocky hit their first red carpet together

The pair caused quite the stir when they walked the red carpet together for the British Fashion Awards. During an interview co-hosted by GQ and Vogue, Rocky revealed to Rihanna that her look that night was his "favorite outfit" of hers. According to Billboard, she designed the Fenty tuxedo he wore.

August 2020: Rihanna and A$AP Rocky collaborate for Fenty Skin

Rocky joined Rihanna's debut Fenty Skin campaign in 2020, sending the Internet into yet another tizzy when the pair began doing press to promote the campaign — notably including their "Face to Face" video interviews for GQ and Vogue, in which they asked each other rapid-fire questions ranging from what their skin is like to the last thing they bought for under $20.

November 2020: PEOPLE confirms Rihanna and A$AP Rocky's romance

A source confirmed that the longtime friends were officially an item in November, after months of romance rumors following Rihanna's split from her boyfriend of three years, billionaire Hassan Jameel, in January 2020.

December 2020: Rihanna and A$AP Rocky vacation in Barbados together

The newly-minted couple took a romantic getaway to Rihanna's native Barbados for their first holiday together. A source told PEOPLE at the time that "A$AP joined her [in Barbados] and they are spending Christmas together with Rihanna's family."

"They've been inseparable the past few weeks," a source told PEOPLE. "It's a new relationship, but they both seem very into it."

The source added, "They've always seemed to have a great time together," continuing, "They have a lot in common."

January 2021: Rihanna Supports A$AP Rocky's Shoe Line

The fashion mogul showed support for her beau on Instagram by posting a sultry shot of herself wearing a pair of heels that the rapper designed. The crystal-embellished gladiator sandals are a collaboration between Amina Muaddi and Rocky's creative agency, AWGE.

April 2021: Rihanna and A$AP Rocky are spotted on a dinner date in Los Angeles

The duo hit up celebrity hot spot Delilah in L.A., reassuring fans that while they may keep their love out of the spotlight, they're indeed still going strong.

May 2021: A$AP Rocky opens up to GQ about his feelings for Rihanna

The "Wild for the Night" rapper got candid about his leading lady in GQ's June/July 2021 cover story, referring to Rihanna as the "love of my life" and "the one."

Describing what it's like to be in a relationship, Rocky said, "So much better. So much better when you got 'the One.' She amounts to probably, like, a million of the other ones."

He continued, "I think when you know, you know. She's the One."

May 2021: Rihanna and A$AP Rocky talk about having kids

Speaking about potentially starting a family one day, Rocky told GQ that it is "in my destiny, absolutely." He added, "I think I'd be an incredible, remarkably overall amazing dad. I would have a very fly child. Very."

Previously, Rihanna told British Vogue in May 2020 that she wants kids without a doubt.

"I'll have kids — three or four of 'em," said the singer, who added without hesitation that she would raise them as a single mother if there wasn't a man in her life.

"I feel like society makes me want to feel like, 'Oh, you got it wrong … ' They diminish you as a mother if there's not a dad in your kids' lives," Rihanna said. "But the only thing that matters is happiness — that's the only healthy relationship between a parent and a child. That's the only thing that can raise a child, truly, is love."

July 2021: Rihanna and A$AP Rocky collaborate once again

The musical duo was spotted working together on an unnamed project, which they were seen filming in New York City. The loved-up pair looked cozy as they held hands and took a stroll through the city.

Days later, Rihanna supported her man backstage at the Rolling Loud Festival in Florida, stepping out for a date night in Miami before returning to N.Y.C. for another night out.

September 13, 2021: Rihanna and A$AP Rocky make their red carpet debut at the Met Gala

The pair picked fashion's biggest night to make their red carpet debut, dressing in coordinated looks for the event. Rihanna arrived in an oversized black sculptural coat dress, while A$AP complimented her with a pop of color in a quilted blanket look.

January 31, 2022: Rihanna announces she's expecting a baby with A$AP Rocky

February 11, 2022: A$AP Rocky and Rihanna coordinate on the red carpet at the Fenty Beauty Universe Event in Los Angeles

April 15, 2022: Designer Amina Muaddi slams 'vile' affair rumor with A$AP Rocky amid Rihanna's pregnancy

Amina Muaddi set the record straight after a Twitter user claimed A$AP Rocky cheated on Rihanna with her amid the singer's pregnancy.

On April 15, the shoe designer shared a statement on Twitter addressing the social media explosion that followed the rumor the day before.

"I've always believed that an unfounded lie spread on social media doesn't deserve any response or clarification, especially one that is so vile," wrote Muaddi, who has previously worked with both the beauty mogul and the rapper.

While Rih is continuing to live her serene, best dressed pregnancy life and I go back to my business — I wish everyone a beautiful Easter weekend!"

A$AP Rocky was arrested and detained by the Los Angeles Police Department on April 20, 2022, in connection with a shooting that took place in November 2021. He was met by LAPD at Los Angeles International Airport after returning from a vacation with his pregnant girlfriend Rihanna in her native Barbados. Rihanna was also present at the scene of the arrest, according to TMZ.

Rocky was charged with the felony of assault with a deadly weapon (firearm). According to court records, his bail was set at $550,000. The rapper allegedly shot a man following an argument. According to the victim, Rocky fled the scene with two other men. After his arrest, Rocky was released from jail later that day.
