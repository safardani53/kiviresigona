What happened to Kortney and Dave Wilson on Masters of Flip?

April 22, 2024
blog

FANS of Masters of Flip loved to watch on screen couple Kortney and Dave Wilson work their magic on real estate.

But some viewers may wonder what happened to the popular pair after the show ended.

Are Kortney and Dave Wilson still together?

Masters of Flip centred on Dave and Kortney Wilson, a married Canadian couple who moved to Nashville, Tennessee, to pursue careers in renovating and flipping houses.

The couple met in the early 2000s through a mutual friend when they were both pursuing music full-time.

He was living in London at the time and was visiting Nashville for a songwriting retreat.

They performed as the country music duo The Wilsons after getting married in 2001.

Eventually, Kortney got her real estate license and decided to focus on buying, flipping and selling homes.

During the early days of the singer’s home improvement career, she starred in Meet the Wilsons with Dave, a reality series about juggling their jobs and parenthood.

They then received further fame in 2015 with their show Masters of Flip,

However, in December 2019, the longtime couple shocked fans when they announced they were getting divorced.

Taking to Instagram they shared this statement: “It is with a very heavy heart that we share with all of you that after nearly 18 years of marriage, we have decided to separate.

“As most of you know, there is so much more to every story and ours is no different.

"What we want you to know most of all is that we have the utmost love and respect for one another.” 

When did Masters of Flip get cancelled?

Fans were left shocked when Masters of Flip was suddenly cancelled in 2018.

The series ended after its fourth season, with the Wilsons moving on to produce and host a new series.

They were lucky enough to land another show called Making it Home with Kortney and Dave - but this ended when they split.

Who has appeared on Masters of Flip with Kortney and Dave Wilson?

Kortney and Dave were the faces of Master of Flip, but were joined by a team of experts.

They were assisted by designer Abby Ellis, and design assistant Jessica Randolph.

Angela Ewing completed the team as she would "stage" the houses, so they looked good for potential buys.

Where are Kortney and Dave Wilson now?

After her split from Dave and the end of Masters of Flip, Kortney was able to find love again.

In March 2022, she announced on Instagram that she and her new man Ryan Velling had got engaged - they went onto wed in 2023.
