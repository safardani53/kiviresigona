REAL Housewives of Beverly Hills star Erika Jayne is “looking forward” to slamming Kyle Richards at the show’s reunion.

The 50-year-old likely won’t hold back after she claimed Kyle is “two-faced literally and figuratively.”

Despite their years-long friendship, sources say Erika isn’t happy with how Kyle, 52, has been talking about her legal drama behind her back on the show.

Noting that Erika views her costars- including Kyle- as hypocrites, an insider told Daily Mail: "The other women say one thing to her face and something completely different behind her back.”

Erika was reportedly shocked that Kyle "turned on her."

The source then claimed Erika "now realizes the warning Lisa Vanderpump gave her about Kyle was true."

"Erika knows that Kyle has two faces both literally and figuratively," the informant added.

KYLE'S COMMENTS

Viewers have watched Kyle shared her thoughts on Erika’s fraud scandal during recent episodes, while a source told The Sun that the 50-year-old is not happy with her friend.

During this season of Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills, the cameras captured the cast having to unravel the massive embezzlement case Erika, 50, is wrapped up in with her husband Tom Girardi. 

Sutton Stracke has been vocal about her questions regarding the case both to Erika’s face and without her around. 

However, Kyle can be heard doubting her friend’s “truth” in confessionals and expressing disgust and shock for the situation without Erika around. 

But when Erika was present, Kyle has been much quieter this season, with many fans even insisting she’s been “silent” in order to “throw the others under the bus.”

Now, a source close to the star told The Sun that Erika is not happy seeing how the women were talking about her behind closed doors as the episodes air. 

"Erika's so upset with her costars watching the show back because they backstab her behind her back,” the insider said. 

They continued: “She’s most furious with Kyle. She feels Kyle was the most two-faced to her and their friendship won't be the same. 

"Kyle's tried to check in since but Erika has had enough. They'll settle their problems at the reunion.”

The insider concluded: “She has a bone to pick with Dorit [Kemsley] too but her focus will be defending herself and calling out Kyle." 

After this week’s wild episode which featured part two of the “dinner party from hell,” Erika is seen exploding at her cast mates for “torturing” her with questions. 

Though most of the ladies had a lot to say, Kyle was quieter and only was seen on the episode speaking up to support Erika.

In her confessionals and with the cast, however, she had a much different tune, which some fans noticed.

After this week's show aired, a viewer on Twitter wrote to Erika: “You don't be fooled!!! KYLE TALKED SMACK ABOUT YOU TOO! SHE WAS IN AGREEMENT WITH THE OTHER LADIES WERE SAYING ABOUT YOU! 

“AND DORIT TALKED ABOUT YOU AND BACK PEDDLED IN FRONT OF THE WORLD!”

'PREPARING FOR BATTLE'

In response, Erika promised: “Don’t worry, we’ll get to that.”

According to the insider, the highly-anticipated reunion will be filming on September 9 and 10 in Los Angeles. 

A source close to the reality star told The Sun that Erika has been “prepping for the reunion for weeks.”

“She’s preparing for battle and she knows she will be hit with tough questions,” the informant claimed of examination from host Andy Cohen. 

The insider explained: “So she is putting a lot of time into her preparation and working closely with her attorney’s to plan how to respond to certain questions. 

“They have briefed her and instructed her what to not say or how to phrase certain things.
