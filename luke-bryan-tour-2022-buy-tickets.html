ASIDE FROM his successful country music career, Luke Bryan appears as a judge on American Idol.

Luke Bryan tour 2022: How can I buy tickets?

Tickets for Luke Bryan's upcoming Raised Up Right 2022 tour can be purchased via his official website and TicketMaster.

Depending on the venue, VIP packages start at $299; whereas General Admission tickets average between around $49 and 400, varying on closeness to the stage.

Joining Luke on tour is Riley Green and Mitchell Tenpenny.
