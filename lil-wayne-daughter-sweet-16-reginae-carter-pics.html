Lil Wayne’s Daughter Celebrates Lavish Sweet 16

It’s too bad My Super Sweet 16 is no longer on the air, because this party definitely would have made the cut!

On Saturday night, Nov. 29, Lil Wayne honored his baby girl, Reginae, with an epic sweet 16 that her friends and family will be talking about for years to come.

The party was all-white with a Cinderella theme, and Reginae arrived in a gorgeous horse-drawn carriage wearing a stunning silver cut-out gown.

Not only did Reginae change into at least 4 different dresses for the evening, but she also had Nicki Minaj perform for everyone!

One of Reginae’s outfits included a white blazer with the words “Young Nae” on the back in gold. She’s a Young Money Princess, for sure!

Reginae Receives 2 Baller Rides From Her Parents

As if her huge party wasn’t enough, Reginae was also surprised at the end with two huge gifts: cars!

Not only was she gifted with a white Ferrari, but she also received a red BMW SUV. You know, so she has options.

The cars were a gift not only from her dad, Lil Wayne, but also her mom, Toya, and her stepfather, Memphitz.

Talk about winning the parental lottery!
