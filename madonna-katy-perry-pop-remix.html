THEY have individually performed for millions at previous Super Bowls, now Madonna and Katy Perry are teaming up for a huge pop collaboration.

And she had plenty of A-list company — with Mary J. Blige, Snoop Dogg, Eminem, Dr Dre, Kendrick Lamar and 50 Cent delivering a jaw-dropping half-time show.

But I can reveal the next big name she will be rubbing shoulders with is Madge, who has enlisted Katy to feature on an upcoming al- bum of re- mixes of her best-known tunes.

The record will mark her milestone of having 50 No1s on Billboard’s Dance Club Songs chart in the US.
