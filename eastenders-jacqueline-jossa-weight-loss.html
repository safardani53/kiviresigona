EastEnders star Jacqueline Jossa shows off amazing weight loss in party dress after soap return

JACQUELINE Jossa looked incredible as she modelled party dresses ahead of her return to EastEnders.

The 31-year-old is launching her latest collection with InTheStyle and showed off her recent weight loss.

The actress has been hitting the gym in preparation for her return to Walford and it’s clearly paid off – judging by her happy and healthy appearance.

Revealing the range of flattering frocks, Jaqueline told her followers: “I feel this is different to my usual party collection.

“It is Fire. I felt amazing in everything and I honestly hope you guys love it as much as me.”

Jacqueline is well loved for her body positivity and has always been candid with her followers about how to get good pics.
