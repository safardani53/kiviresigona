HYBE Will Be Having a Multi-Label Worldwide Audition In The US

August 06, 2024
blog

Seven of HYBE’s music companies will be hunting for talent in the United States this upcoming month, according to the K-pop agency. Anyone aged 11 to 19 can apply in three categories: voice, rap, and dance, irrespective of gender. Global talent, notably from North America, is anticipated to take active participation in the tryouts, according to the firm.

The multi-label auditions will be conducted in Las Vegas on April 8, 9, 15, and 16. The agency’s music firms, Big Hit Music, Source Music, HYBE Labels Japan, HYBE America, Pledis Entertainment, Belift Lab, and KOZ Entertainment, are among the seven that will be participating in the auditions. Big Hit Music, which oversees BTS, is one of the company’s subsidiaries.

HYBE Global Audition 2022- How To Apply And Qualifying Requirements

Applications can be submitted via a link on the official Instagram account or YouTube channel that connects to a Google form. The closing date is April 4, 2022.

Entering the typical data like name, email, performance category (singing, rapping, dancing), nationality, gender, birth year, phone number, social media profile links, and a front-facing photo are all required.

Applicants can also attach the URL to a clip of their performance. Global residents of both genders, between the ages of 11 and 19, are able to sign up for the agency’s multi-label audition.

In a statement, the business stated:

“Young talent to be identified in the auditions will receive an opportunity to grow to be worldwide musicians with aid from specialists in various labels that have world-class capabilities.”

Stay informed on the agency audition’s official Instagram account and website for additional updates.

More About The K-Pop Agency

In May, the K-pop agency will introduce a new female group with Miyawaki Sakura and Kim Chaewon. The label revealed its debut on March 21, 2022. Bang Si-hyuk, the founder of Big Hit Music and several companies, personally greeted the new female group members through a social media post.

For K-pop lovers throughout the world, the possibility of joining this agency is their goal since the record company is home to many of the best music groups in history, including Grammy-nominated global boy group BTS from Big Hit Music.
