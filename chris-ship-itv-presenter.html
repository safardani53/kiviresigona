Who is ITV presenter Chris Ship?

July 07, 2024
blog

ITV ARE known for their impressive coverage of the Royal family, which includes the sad passing of Queen Elizabeth.

One man who is integral in the team is their Royal Editor Chris Ship.

Who is Chris Ship?

Chris was brought up in Southampton, and educated at Bellemoor School for Boys.

After this he attended the University of Sussex, where he studied Media and French.

Chris is best known for reporting on the Royal Family across TV and digital for ITV News.

He is also the the host of The Royal Rota, the ITV News podcast.

You can follow Chris and his royal updates on his Twitter handle @chrisshipitv.

He also keeps fans up-to-date on his Instagram page @chris.ship.royal.

Chris will be key in leading ITV's Queen Elizabeth II's funeral coverage.

He has reported on all major Royal Family occasions, including the 2018 wedding of Prince Harry and Meghan Markle.

What TV shows has he presented?

Chris originally joined ITV News as a political correspondent.

In 2008 he was promoted to senior political correspondent for the ITN-produced service.

Since early 2009, he has been a presenter of the ITV Weekend News.

Chris was given a new title of Deputy Political Editor in 2012, then in December 2016 it was announced he was the new Royal Editor, replacing the retiring Tim Ewart.

Since September 2019 he has also been a relief presenter of the ITV Lunchtime News.

Is Chris married and does he have children?

Chris keeps his personal life out of the spotlight, and is private about his marital status.
