Know About Dancing With The Stars Lindsay Arnold's Salary & Net Worth! 

While the exact salary and net worth of Dancing With The Stars' Lindsay remain unknown, it has long been reported that every participant on every season of Dancing With the Stars gets paid about $125,000 for joining the cast.

Those who appear on the show for more than the first two episodes get a bigger payday whereas those who pass over four episodes earn an additional $10K on top of their signing bonus.

After that, the price is raised to $20K, and $30K for starring in subsequent seasons. The finalists on each season are given $50K.

Furthermore, according to Zip Recruiter, the average salary for a Professional Dancer Across the U.S. is $69,922 a year.

In 2013, Lindsay joined the show and was paired with the professional boxer, Victor Ortiz. At that time, she was the second youngest dancer on the show. 

On her first season, she made it up to the sixth week. However, she continued participating until the 25th season. With her astonishing performance, she became more popular.

In the 21st season, she ended up as the second runner-up in the finale. The next year, she reached the semis. In the 23rd installment of the show, she ended up third, in the 24th, she came second and finally in the 25th season of Dancing with the stars, she won along with singer and actor, Jordan Fisher.
