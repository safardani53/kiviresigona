THE View host Sara Haines has taken to social media to show her make-up-free skin in a new behind-the-scenes video of her in her morning show dressing room.

On Tuesday, Sara recently shared a clip of herself stripping off her on-air makeup as she got ready "to go home" after a day on The View on Instagram.

Sara slid a headband on her head as she spoke at the beginning of the video, pulling her hair completely out of her face.

The 45-year-old explained in the flick that the "first thing" she did when she got "done with shooting" an episode of the talk show was "take off all" her "makeup."

"I am semi-obsessed with skincare and taking care of my own skin," Sara said in the social media video.

The ABC anchor began her makeup removal routine using Shu Uemura's Ultimate8 Cleansing Oil to loosen up the cosmetic products used on her face, making it easier to come off.
