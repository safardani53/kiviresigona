10 Best High Waisted Jeans For Women 2021 | The Sun UK

August 08, 2024
blog

HIGH waisted jeans are a must-have as they contour your curves, pull in your waist and you never have to worry about your knickers rising out the top of them.

Thankfully we left muffin-top spilling low-risers in the early noughties and as vintage fashion flourishes, high-waisted denim has never been so popular.

They're the jeans that have featured in countless iconic photographs of Marilyn Monroe. The Hollywood legend showed how a high-waisted silhouette hugs your body in all the right places.

Fast forward to today and style icons are continuing to prove they’re the coolest, most flattering trousers to wear.

Supermodels worldwide, like Gigi Hadid and Kendall Jenner, style them for everything from off-duty looks to boujee dinner dates.

Whether you want some comfy mom jeans or this season’s favourite straight-leg style - there are a huge variety of cuts to choose from.

To make everything a bit easier for you, we’ve picked out our favourite high-waisted jeans for women that you can buy now. Thank us later.

What body type looks best in high waisted jeans?

You don't have to be a supermodel like Kenny to pull off this trend.

If you're vertically challenged, skinny jeans work wonders for creating the impression that you have long legs - as long as they aren't wide-leg!

Curvy girls, you can rock a pair too- just look for stretchy skinnies with back pockets centred directly in the middle.

If you feel you lack curves, look for a pair of skinny jeans with back pockets that sit higher to fake a Kardashian booty.

Insecure about your tummy? Fear not, skinny jeans or ULTRA high-rise jeans banish muffin top.

How do you style high waisted jeans?

Don't overthink when you want to style high waisted jeans.

To create simple, but chick outfit you can choose anything from Breton tops to Metallica's logo.
