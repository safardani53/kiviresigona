Sony's official PS4 DualShock 4 wireless controller down to just 35.99 with this deal

February 16, 2024
blog

SONY PlayStation 4 owners on the hunt for an extra PS4 DualShock 4 wireless controller can grab it for ten quid less right now thanks to this fresh Argos deal.

The joypad is available online and in store in four shiny colours ( black, white, blue, and red) for just £35.99 – a tenner less than its normal cost of £45.99.

That's the lowest price ever for the wireless joypad at Argos.

For those in need of a refresher, the PS4 DualShock 4 wireless controller continues the classic design of Sony's PlayStation joypads, with some cool upgrades to boot.

Chief among them is its wireless capability, so you never have to worry about bothersome cables getting in the way of your best plays again.

There's also that cool blue light bar at the top , which flickers in different colours depending on the game you're playing.

Fire up Grand Theft Auto 5, for example, and it will pulse red and blue whenever the cops are in hot pursuit and change hues depending on the character you choose.

Meanwhile, Sony swapped out the start and select buttons for share and option keys that let you upload gameplay on to live-streaming services like Twitch and access in-game menus.

The Argos deal lands just in time for E3 2018, where Sony is expected to focus on four tantalising new titles: Spider-Man, The Last of Us Part II, Death Stranding, and Ghost of Tsushima.

Plus, it will also come in handy for local multiplayer fans, especially those that want to re-create World Cup matches on FIFA 18 with their mates – that way England can win every time.
