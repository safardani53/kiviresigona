How much is a Costco membership?

If you want to visit Costco's food court, you need to be a member.

There are three types of Costco memberships.

A gold and business membership is $60 per year.

An executive membership is $120 per year which includes an annual two percent reward on purchases.

For example, if you spend $250 a month for a year that would come out to $3,000. You would receive an annual reward of $60.

The gold membership includes the free household card and is valid at all Costco locations.

For the business card, it's relatively the same as the gold although businesses are able to purchase items for resale.
