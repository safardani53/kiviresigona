Career

Slater, the son of a bait-store proprietor, by the age of 10, started competing and won his first division U.S. championship title.

After two years, he competed in the junior division at the world amateur championships (third) in England and the Pacific Cup junior championship in Australia (first).

In 1990, Slater turned professional but struggled during his first two years on the professional tour.

After winning his first professional tour event, the Rip Curl Pro, in France, he took a break from competitive surfing at the end of 1998 before returning to the world pro tour in 2002.

He also won major surfing league championships like the Quicksilver pro-Australian Open.

Winning a total of 55 world surf league contests and holding the title “The world championship holder” from 1994-1998, he has received several awards throughout his surfing career.

Besides his surfing career, he has also been in several movies and TV shows.

Kelly slater is in the second season of new docuseries about surfers ‘Make or Break,’ an eight-part season, which offers behind-the-scenes access to the internationally-recognized World Surf League champions Kelly Slater, Stephanie Gilmore, Filipe Toledo, and others released by Apple TV+.

They compete for the top title at the Men’s and Women’s World Surf League Championship Tour.

The company will release four episodes of season two on Friday, February 17, 2023, followed by another four episodes on February 24, 2023. It takes viewers on location for the 2022 competition to surfing locations worldwide.
