Kanye West nominated for 2021 Grammy Award after he PEED on his past trophies as Beyonce and Taylor

June 04, 2024
blog

KANYE West has been nominated for the 2021 Grammy Awards - even after he peed on his past trophies in a shocking video on social media.

The rapper is up for one nonimation, as his album Jesus Is King is named in the best contemporary Christian music album category.

The nod comes two months after he shared a video which appears to show him peeing on his grammy, after placing it in the toilet bowl.

The rappers video came after he had hit out at the record industry, and put record label Universal on blast, alleging he had signed over 10 contracts with the label and that they had tried to "bury him alive."

Elsewhere, it was good news for Taylor Swift, however, who has six nominations to her name, including album of the year for the first time since 2016 when she won with 1989.

The popstar has been nominated for her surprise eighth album Folklore, which is nominated in album of the year and pop vocal album.

She is also nominated for pop solo performance and song of the year for Cardigan, and best pop duo for Exile with Bon Iver.

Her original song for Cats, Beautiful Ghosts, is also nominated for best song written for visual media.

The 30-year-old has tough competition in the album of the year category, with Dua Lipa, close friends Haim, Coldplay and Post Malone all nominated.

Beyoncé snagged nine nods including record of the year for Black Parade, and down-ballot categories such as best R&B Performance and best rap song.

Noah Cyrus is up for best new artist, alongside Megan Thee Stallion, Ingrid Andress, Phoebe Bridgers and Doja Cat.
