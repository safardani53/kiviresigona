Jessica Dean's Enchanting Biography Reveals Supportive Family

Born in Little Rock, Arkansas, on 22 August 1985, Jessica Dean holds an American nationality and is of white ethnical background.

Her Journey To CBS 3

After the graduations, she started her career as a reporter for the station KNWA/KFTA located in Fort Smith, Arkansas; some of her responsibilities during this time included being an editor, anchor, reporter and photographer-and although she didn’t like photography-she was thankful for the numerous roles she had the chance to incorporate within the early phases of her career.

Aside from her work with KNWA and the station KATV located in Little Rock, she also garnered more working experiences from NBC affiliate KARK-TV, Little Rock in 2011.

Having been instrumental ever since her early career, she finally got the chance to join CBS 3 in 2013. She joined CBS 3 as the weekday evening anchor, often alongside Chris May in the station’s various evening newscasts.

The Arkansas-native then moved to Philadelphia, and eventually become one of the anchors of “Eyewitness News” alongside Ukee Washington, and of “the Digital Brief,” a part of the channel’s online website CBSPhilly.com.
