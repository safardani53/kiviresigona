EVER since she teamed up with Peacocks, Louise Redknapp’s range has been flying off of shelves. 

And the singer’s latest collaboration with the high-street fashion brand is no different – as shoppers say it’s perfect for the party season.

Louise, 49, recently unveiled her festive capsule collection which features a line-up of on-trend items.

Introducing it to their customers, Peacocks said: “The new Louise Redknapp capsule collection, featuring PU leather and velvet separates as well as statement party dresses and tops, classic blazers and sleek wide leg trousers, will ensure you’re ready for the party season ahead!”

True to their word, there are plenty of eye-catching ensembles and more sequins that you can imagine – from a grey sparkly midi skirt for £20 to a pair of £28 black sequin trousers. 

The range also includes the likes of a sparkly top for £20 and a green sequin shirt for £28.

But now Louise has shared her top pick that should fly straight to number one on your wish list – hailing her LBD sheer lace dress as “a must have.” 

The mum-of-two, who wore the flattering number on a recent night out, took to her Instagram account to shout about the dress she said is perfect for the festive season. 

The Eternal singer turned solo star revealed: “One of my favourite pieces from my latest Peacocks edit is available online and in store now.

“This black lace dress is a must have for the party season.”

And it didn’t take long for people to stand up and take notice of the dress that is £35. 

One person wrote on her Instagram post: “I love this look!”

A second added: “This is a gorgeous dress.” 

While a third said: “This is lovely!” 

Since, fellow fashion fans have gone wild for the simple but effective dress that is described as “a gorgeous black midi dress.”

The garment’s description continues: “Crafted with black laser cut lace featuring three quarter sleeves, a high neckline, a black slip and a scallop detail hemline – it's perfect for the party season.” 

The dress is currently sold out online, but is expected to restock soon, with Peacocks asking interested customers to provide their email address so they can tell them when it’s in.

Meanwhile, the lucky ones who have already snapped it up have been keen to leave their thoughts.

One person wrote a glowing review, as they typed: “Classy Dress and looks lovely on. I would recommend.”
