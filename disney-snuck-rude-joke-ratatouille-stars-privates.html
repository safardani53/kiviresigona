Disney snuck a VERY rude joke into Ratatouille about star's privates - but did you spot it?

February 25, 2024
blog

IT may be one of the most popular Disney films of all time, but Ratatouille has had a dirty joke hiding in plain sight for years.

The beloved animated feature tells the story of Remy the rat who has big dreams of becoming a celebrated chef, supported by his cheeky sidekick, Alfredo Linguini.

And it's chef Linguini who is responsible for the dirty joke in the 2007 film.

Remy is helping Linguini keep his job at top-rated restaurant by secretly giving him tips on how to make the best food.

Linguini eventually confesses to fellow cook, Colette, that he has a special helper.

"I have this tiny … little … little …" he starts telling her.

Colette looks down at his crotch, to seal the deal on the cheeky innuendo.

But it's not the only naughty moment which made it in to the Pixar animated film.

One angry customer is heard saying, "If I don’t love it, I don’t swallow."

The dirty jokes come as adults who grew up with Disney films have watched them again years late and realised what was happening, having missed the naughty fun as kids. 

One example is in the Oscar winning film Aladdin.

The Genie, voiced by the late Robin Williams, helps Aladdin and Princess Jasmine prepare for their wedding.

However, before the big day, Aladdin reunites with his long-lost father Cassim, who is obsessed with finding the Hand of Midas, which turns everything it touches to gold.

Chaos then ensues, and at one point, Aladdin and Jasmine are standing on top of an elephant when one of the villains jabs it in the rear with something sharp.
