Laverne Cox Got a Christmas Card From Beyonce, Couldn't Handle It

May 11, 2024
blog

Yes Laverne, there is a Santa Claus! Orange Is the New Black star Laverne Cox got one special Christmas gift this week from none other than Beyonce Knowles.  

The Emmy-nominated actress, 30, shared a photo via Instagram on Friday, Dec. 19 of the holiday present that was sent by Queen Bey, 33. The multi-pic shot features a note reading, "HAPPY HOLIDAYS Love Beyonce," a green sweatshirt emblazoned with the "Partition" lyrics "cake by the pound," and a shot of the singer in a Christmas sweater.

"When I opened this holiday card a few days ago and read Love @beyonce I literally fell to the floor," Cox captioned the image. "I was on the floor of my apartment. I have witnesses. Love the #CakeByThePound sweatshirt. Thanks Bey for the gift. I have had an awesome year and this is certainly a highlight."
