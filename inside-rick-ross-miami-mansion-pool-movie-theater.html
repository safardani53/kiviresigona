Inside Rick Ross' massive $3.5M Miami mansion featuring resort-style pool, movie theater &amp; NINE car

September 29, 2024
blog

INSIDE Rick Ross’ massive $3.5m Miami mansion, which features a resort-style pool, movie theater and a NINE car garage. 

The rapper, 45, paid all CASH for the stunning home – set over 7,361-square-foot – with four bedrooms property, an indoor bar area and a yoga room. 

Rick is no stranger to incredible homes, he also owns a 45,000-square-foot mansion in Fayetteville, Georgia. 

The fancy place is so spectacular it was used as a stand-in PALACE for the new movie Coming 2 America, starring Eddie Murphy.

And now Ross has added another residence to his portfolio with the purchase of this luxury Florida estate. 

Located west of Fort Lauderdale in Southwest Ranches, the music mogul parted with $3.5m of cold, hard cash just four days after the house went on the market. 

Listed by Landmark Ranch Estates, Ross snapped up the property from former NBA star Amare Stoudemire, 38, in what’s being called as the “fastest closing” in the company’s history.

Kim Knausz with ONE Sotheby’s International Realty represented Stoudemire while Ross was represented by his sister and agent, Tawanda Roberts.

Ross’s new home is set over 7,361-square-foot and has four massive bedrooms, five and a half bathrooms, a hidden movie theater, two offices, a yoga room and an indoor bar area with a pool table. 

The master bedroom also features a chandelier-clad closet and ensuite luxury bathroom with a tub that some may consider a small pool.

Outside in the sprawling grounds, the rapper has plenty of space to enjoy the sunshine by his very own resort-style pool and waterfall. 

When it comes to getting the family over for a cookout, Rick’s home can deliver with a complete outdoor kitchen including a grill, all surrounded by a sweeping deck. 

Speaking about his new purchase, Ross told PEOPLE: “It's a sprawling estate in a very secluded area. From the moment I stepped inside, I appreciated the high ceilings and rich detail.”

Elsewhere on the estate there is a porte-cochere, which is essentially a porch where vehicles stop to set down passengers, and a nine-car garage. 

There’s also a two-bedroom, one-bath guest house that offers another 1,314 square feet of space, which has its own kitchen and a living room.

Discussing his 45,000 square-foot, grandiose home in Georgia, Ross revealed to Variety that he was very happy for it to be used as a set in Coming 2 America.

“They changed the wallpaper in the dining room so I asked them to keep it up there,” Ross explained.

“They also created that huge dining room table for a dining scene that seats 50-60 people, and they left that for me as a gift. It’s humungous.”

The film used five key locations in the house – the entrance foyer, two large rooms with 18-foot ceilings, the dining room and a bedroom, which was Ross’ own.
