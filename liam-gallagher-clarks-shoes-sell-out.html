Liam Gallagher's 130 Clarks shoes sell out in minutes and are being flogged online for double the

February 11, 2024
blog

FANS have gone mad for Liam Gallagher’s new shoes — with Clarks selling out in minutes.

Pairs of the LG Rambler — which the 50-year-old former Oasis rocker helped design — are now being offered online for more than double the original £130 price.

Some fans were angered to see the suede shoes listed for up to £300 on eBay last night.

One raged on a social media page dedicated to fashion: “This makes me sick.

"People just buying them to make a profit or people who want to collect them but never wear them.

"Get them on your feet.”

Shoemaker Clarks said Roll With It star Liam has been wearing its designs since the 1990s.
